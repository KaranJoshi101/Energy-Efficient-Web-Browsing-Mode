Reduce Carbon Emission

Reduces carbon emissions by optimizing your browser's energy and resource usage. Lowers CPU and memory consumption, reducing the power needed to keep your device running efficiently.

Dark Mode

Applies a darker color scheme to webpages. On devices with OLED or AMOLED screens, it saves energy by turning off pixels for dark areas.

Ad-Block

Blocks intrusive or resource-heavy advertisements on websites. By preventing ads from loading, it reduces data usage, improves page loading speeds, and minimizes CPU and memory consumption.

ABOUT US

The internet consumes around 416 terawatts of electricity per year, more than the UK's annual consumption (IEA) because users lack tools to manage energy usage in daily browsing, despite a growing demand for sustainable tech.

The Energy Efficient Web Browsing Mode is a Chrome extension designed to optimize browserbehaviour and minimize energy consumption during web browsing. Battery Mode Detection Auto-Blocking Ads Real-Time Alerts This extension intelligently manages browser settings and operations based on user activity andthe content being viewed, providing an eco-friendly browsing experience.
